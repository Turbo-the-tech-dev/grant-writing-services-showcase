## Budget Narrative Prompts

### Personnel

- List each position funded.
- State FTE or hours committed.
- Explain salary basis and why the role is necessary.

### Fringe Benefits

- Identify the approved fringe rate or actual basis.
- Note what is included in the rate.

### Contractual

- Name each contractor or describe the outsourced role.
- Explain procurement or selection logic if required.

### Travel

- Break out transportation, lodging, meals, and registration.
- Tie travel directly to project delivery.

### Equipment and Supplies

- Separate capital items from consumables.
- Explain unit cost and quantity.

### Other and Indirect

- Note occupancy, communications, evaluation, or audit costs as needed.
- State the indirect rate basis or explain de minimis treatment.
