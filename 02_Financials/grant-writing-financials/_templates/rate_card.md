# Grant Writing Rate Card

## Retainer Tiers

| Tier | Monthly Fee | Best For | Includes |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Foundation | 1500 | Early-stage nonprofits | Funding calendar, prospect research, one monthly strategy call |
| Growth | 2500 | Organizations with active submissions | Strategy support, two proposal rounds per month, deadline management |
| Institutional | 4500 | Teams pursuing larger or government grants | Full pipeline oversight, multi-stakeholder coordination, reporting support |

## Project Fee Menu

| Service | Typical Fee Range | Notes |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Grant readiness assessment | 750 - 1500 | Includes document review and action memo |
| Prospect research package | 900 - 1800 | Based on number of funders |
| Foundation proposal | 1500 - 4000 | Depends on complexity and attachments |
| Federal or state proposal support | 4000 - 12000 | Scope should be defined in a custom SOW |
| Grant budget development | 500 - 2500 | Standalone or bundled |
| Reporting narrative support | 400 - 1500 | Based on length and data cleanup required |

## Pricing Notes

- Rush work should include a premium.
- Complex collaborations should be scoped separately.
- Travel, translation, design, and evaluation support should be billed separately unless expressly included.
