# Grant Writing Financials

Real on-disk financial system for a grant writing business.

## Structure

- `01_revenue/` - revenue tracker, win/loss log, retainer tracker, invoices by year
- `02_expenses/` - expense log, subscription tracker, receipts by year
- `03_tax/` - quarterly tax worksheet and annual folders
- `04_grant-budgets/` - client budget template plus active/submitted folders
- `05_contracts/` - service agreement template plus active/archived folders
- `06_reporting/` - monthly P&L template and annual review template
- `07_banking/` - bank statements and reconciliation drop-in folder
- `_templates/` - invoice template and rate card

## Setup Notes

- Duplicate templates before using them with client-specific names.
- Save invoices, receipts, signed contracts, and statements into the year folders as you go.
- Update the CSV trackers weekly to keep tax and reporting clean.
