# California STEP Prompt Pack

These prompts are tailored to the California-administered STEP workflow and the local codex structure in this repository.

## 1. STEP Opportunity Fit Check

You are a grant strategist reviewing a California STEP opportunity for a small business client in Gardena, California. Assess fit based on these criteria: California operations, one-year business history, SBA size standard fit, 51 percent U.S. content, export readiness, ability to pay upfront costs, and clarity of target international market. Return:

- fit score from 1 to 10
- top 3 strengths
- top 3 risks
- missing documents
- recommendation: pursue now, prepare first, or skip

Use only the facts provided. If information is missing, flag it explicitly.

## 2. STEP Eligibility Memo

Draft a concise internal eligibility memo for a California STEP application. Summarize whether the client appears eligible, what facts support that conclusion, and what evidence still needs to be collected. Keep the memo structured for storage in `01_PreAward/Opportunity_Research/`.

## 3. STEP Export Voucher Narrative

You are an expert export grant writer. Draft a California STEP export voucher narrative for this client. Explain the export opportunity, why the business is ready for international expansion, how the reimbursable activity supports export growth, and what measurable outcomes are expected. Maintain a professional voice, avoid unsupported claims, and flag any missing evidence with bracketed placeholders.

## 4. STEP Event Justification

Draft a justification for STEP-supported participation in an international trade show or trade mission. Cover why the event matches the client's export strategy, the market opportunity, expected buyer or partner outcomes, and the specific business benefits of attendance.

## 5. STEP Digital Expansion Plan

Draft a focused plan showing how an online business will use STEP-supported activities for international expansion. Address website localization, export-compliant e-commerce adjustments, international digital marketing, translation needs, and target-market customer acquisition.

## 6. STEP Compliance Check

Review this draft against California STEP guidance and return:

- likely compliance strengths
- possible compliance gaps
- unsupported claims
- missing documentation
- questions that must be answered before submission

Do not assume facts that are not in the source materials.

## 7. STEP Reimbursement File Checklist

Create a post-submission checklist for a California STEP reimbursement file. Include approval records, invoices, proof of payment, performance evidence, export activity documentation, and final naming/storage recommendations using `YYYY-MM-DD_Description_Version.ext`.
