# Website Content Checklist

## Brand Inputs

- Final business name
- Logo or wordmark
- Primary service area
- Phone number and business email
- Booking link
- Business address or service-area-only preference

## Trust Inputs

- Verified total funding secured
- Verified success or submission rate
- Client logos approved for public display
- Testimonials with written permission
- Certifications, memberships, or professional credentials
- Community involvement notes

## Service Inputs

- Service tiers and starting prices
- Ideal client profile for each service
- Turnaround times
- Required client materials
- Refund, cancellation, and scope-change policies

## Launch Checks

- All links tested
- Forms route to the correct inbox or CRM
- Mobile layout reviewed
- Accessibility pass completed
- Local SEO title tags and meta descriptions written
- Privacy policy and terms added
