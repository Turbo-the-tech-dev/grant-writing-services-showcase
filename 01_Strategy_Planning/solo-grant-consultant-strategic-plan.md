# Solo Grant Writing Consultant Strategic Plan

## Core Philosophy

As a solo operator, the primary constraint is time, not capability. The purpose of systems and processes is to convert expertise into repeatable assets so delivery becomes more consistent, scalable, and defensible.

## Strategic Throughline

Across every planning horizon, the core objective is the same:

- reduce reactive work
- standardize repeatable tasks
- protect capacity
- improve decision quality
- build reusable intellectual property

## Next 90 Days: Build The Foundation

The near-term goal is to install the minimum operating infrastructure required to run the business deliberately instead of reactively.

### Client Intake And Qualification System

Create a standard intake form and a simple qualification rubric that scores:

- mission alignment
- budget readiness
- deadline feasibility
- funder relationship readiness

Target outcome:

- fewer poor-fit clients
- clearer go or no-go decisions
- faster proposal scoping

### Master Template Library

Audit previous grant work and extract reusable language and structures, organized by section type rather than by client.

Core categories:

- organizational background
- needs statement language
- impact and outcomes language
- budget justification language
- logic model structures
- evaluation plan language

Target outcome:

- less drafting from scratch
- faster first-draft production
- stronger consistency across deliverables

### Project Management Infrastructure

Use one primary project management tool and define a consistent grant lifecycle:

`prospect -> LOI -> full proposal -> submission -> follow-up -> reporting`

Target outcome:

- every active opportunity tracked in one place
- reduced deadline risk
- easier workload visibility

### Deadline And Deliverable Calendar

Build a rolling six-month calendar of:

- recurring funder deadlines
- client deliverables
- reporting obligations
- internal draft milestones

Target outcome:

- proactive planning
- fewer last-minute rushes
- better capacity allocation

### Rate And Scope Standardization

Define service tiers with standard timelines, deliverables, and pricing logic.

Suggested service lines:

- LOI only
- full proposal
- retainer grant management
- reporting and compliance support

Target outcome:

- faster quoting
- clearer client expectations
- improved profitability

## Six-Month Roadmap: Systematize Client Delivery

Once the foundation is in place, the focus shifts to making delivery faster, clearer, and easier to repeat.

### Discovery Process

Build a structured kickoff process with:

- a standard 60 to 90 minute discovery agenda
- required pre-call client homework
- a document request checklist

Target outcome:

- less follow-up chasing
- stronger source material
- better proposal quality early in the process

### Funder Research Database

Build a proprietary research database that tracks:

- funder priorities
- giving history
- grant ranges
- deadlines
- eligibility signals
- relationship notes

Target outcome:

- faster prospecting
- better strategic fit analysis
- accumulation of durable competitive advantage

### Writing Process Workflow

Document the actual writing workflow step by step:

`research -> outline -> first draft -> self-review -> client review -> revision -> final polish -> submission checklist`

Target outcome:

- more accurate project estimates
- easier batching
- cleaner future delegation

### Client Communication Cadence

Create reusable templates for recurring communications:

- kickoff confirmation
- source material request
- draft delivery
- revision request
- submission confirmation
- award or denial follow-up

Target outcome:

- less cognitive overhead
- more consistent client experience
- fewer avoidable communication gaps

### Win/Loss Tracking

Track every submission with:

- client
- sector
- funder
- amount requested
- amount awarded
- result
- feedback

Target outcome:

- evidence-based positioning
- better niche selection
- more credible business development messaging

## Annual Plan: Optimize And Protect Capacity

At the annual horizon, the priority is stability, leverage, and controlled growth.

### Retainer Model Development

Shift a meaningful share of revenue toward recurring services such as:

- grant pipeline development
- ongoing proposal support
- reporting and compliance
- grant calendar management

Target outcome:

- smoother revenue
- lower sales volatility
- stronger client retention

### Niche Deepening

Use win/loss and delivery data to identify the strongest sectors, funder types, or client profiles.

Target outcome:

- sharper positioning
- higher-quality opportunities
- less price competition

### Subcontractor Bench

Identify trusted support partners for:

- research
- editing
- budgeting
- technical review

Target outcome:

- overflow capacity without fixed payroll
- improved resilience during peak demand

### Annual Client Review Process

Create a repeatable annual review for retainer clients covering:

- what was submitted
- what was won
- what was declined
- current pipeline
- next-year strategy

Target outcome:

- deeper retention
- clearer strategy alignment
- natural upsell opportunities

### Personal Capacity Audit

Track time by project type and review quarterly to determine:

- highest-value work
- low-leverage tasks to reduce or delegate
- maximum sustainable workload

Target outcome:

- better margin protection
- lower burnout risk
- stronger long-term decision making

## Multi-Year Vision: Build An Asset, Not Only A Job

The long-term goal is to create a business that has value beyond daily personal labor.

### Intellectual Property Development

Turn repeatable frameworks into named methodologies, templates, and internal systems.

Examples:

- funding readiness framework
- proposal quality checklist
- grant opportunity fit score

Target outcome:

- stronger differentiation
- future training or productization opportunities

### Thought Leadership

Build consistent sector visibility through:

- newsletter content
- LinkedIn publishing
- workshops
- conference speaking

Target outcome:

- inbound demand
- credibility
- brand value beyond one-to-one service work

### Digital Revenue Streams

Develop offerings that are not fully dependent on billable hours:

- template packs
- trainings
- courses
- memberships
- internal grant capacity toolkits

Target outcome:

- revenue diversification
- better leverage
- resilience against client workload fluctuations

### Exit Options

Keep optionality open for:

- lifestyle business stability
- partnership
- small agency buildout
- sale of systems, brand, or IP

Target outcome:

- strategic flexibility
- stronger enterprise value

## Suggested Operating Metrics

Track a few core measures so strategy can be reviewed against actual performance:

- qualified leads per month
- proposal conversion rate
- average revenue per client
- retainer revenue percentage
- on-time submission rate
- average hours by proposal type
- win rate by niche

2. Build the first version of the template library.
3. Choose one project management system and migrate active work into it.
