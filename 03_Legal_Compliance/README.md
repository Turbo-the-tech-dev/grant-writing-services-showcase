# Grant Writing Legal Toolkit

A practical legal and compliance document set tailored for a solo California-based grant writing consultant.

## Contents

```text
grant-writing-legal/
├── 01_business-formation/    LLC/DBA tracker, SAM.gov fields, state portal links
├── 02_contracts/             Master service agreement, change order template, active log
├── 03_intellectual-property/ IP ownership policy, mutual NDA template, registered IP folder
├── 04_compliance/            Federal and California compliance references
├── 05_insurance/             E&O, general liability, cyber coverage tracker, incident log
├── 06_subcontractors/        Subcontractor agreement, 1099 filing checklist
├── 07_disputes/              Escalation process, past-due invoice sequence, demand letter template
├── 08_privacy-data/          Data handling policy, breach response protocol, AI tool caution notes
└── _templates/               Ethics reference materials and decision tools
```

## Highlights

The master service agreement goes beyond a basic freelance contract. It includes a contingency fee prohibition, data security terms, IP ownership tied to full payment, and a limitation of liability cap.

The California compliance materials address AB 5 and the ABC Test for both your own contractor status and any subcontractors you engage.

The privacy and data materials include guidance on handling PII and using AI writing tools responsibly.

## Important Notice

These documents are templates and reference materials only. They are not legal advice. Have a licensed attorney review them before using them in active client matters.
