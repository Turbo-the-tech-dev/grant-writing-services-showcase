# Maritime Towing & Marine AI Grants (2026)

For businesses involved in tug, towboat, or barge operations.

## NOAA Ocean Technology Transition Program
- **Funding:** Up to **$400,000 per year** for up to three years.
- **Focus:** Transitioning marine sensors, AI data visualization, and platform enhancements from research to full operations.
- **Cycle:** Active for FY 2026–2029.

## Maine Marine Innovation Grants
- **Funding:** Up to **$4,000** per year.
- **Focus:** Marine electrification, including electric outboards and shoreside charging infrastructure.
