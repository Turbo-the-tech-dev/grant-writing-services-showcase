# Tax Credits & Incentives (2026)

Financial incentives for clean energy and EV adoption.

## Commercial Clean Vehicle Credit (45W)
- **Credit:** Up to **$40,000** tax credit.
- **Focus:** Medium/heavy-duty EVs (over 14,000 lbs), including electric tow trucks.

## Alternative Fuel Infrastructure Credit (30C)
- **Credit:** 30% tax credit (up to **$100,000**).
- **Focus:** EV charging equipment installed before **June 30, 2026**.
