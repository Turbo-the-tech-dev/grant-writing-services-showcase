# Federal R&D & AI Technology Grants (2026)

These programs fund the development of new AI-driven hardware and software for the transportation and energy sectors.

## DOE "Genesis Mission" (AI-Enabled Innovation)
- **Funding:** Part of a **$293 million** initiative to accelerate AI-enabled breakthroughs.
- **Focus:** Advanced manufacturing, energy systems, and autonomous laboratories.
- **Deadline:** Phase I applications were due **April 28, 2026**; Phase II letters of intent were also due in late April.

## U.S. DOT SBIR FY2026 Phase I
- **Funding:** Up to **$200,000** for 6-month feasibility studies.
- **Relevant Topics:** 
    - **26-FH1:** Edge AI-V2X solutions for congestion and traffic management.
    - **26-FH2:** Automated vehicle-mounted inspection systems.
    - **Freight Intelligence:** AI for freight corridor predictive analytics.
- **Deadline:** Pre-solicitation closed May 29, 2026. The formal solicitation period is **June 3 – July 7, 2026**.

## NSF SBIR/STTR (Artificial Intelligence & Mobility)
- **Funding:** Phase I up to **$305,000**.
- **Focus:** "Mobility" (MO) topic covers land, air, and sea-based movement of goods. "Artificial Intelligence" (AI) topic covers deep learning and AI-based hardware.
- **Deadline:** Rolling deadlines (Project Pitches accepted year-round).
