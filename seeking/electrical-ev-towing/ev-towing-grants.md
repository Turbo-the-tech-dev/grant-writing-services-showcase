# Electrical & Heavy-Duty Towing Grants (2026)

These grants focus on the transition to electric vehicles (EVs) and the infrastructure required for heavy-duty towing operations.

## EPA Clean Heavy-Duty Vehicles Grant Program
- **Funding:** A **$1 billion** initiative funded by the Inflation Reduction Act.
- **Focus:** Replacing Class 6 and 7 vocational vehicles (including tow trucks) with zero-emission models.
- **Status:** Check for 2026 "Notice of Funding Opportunity" (NOFO) updates.

## California HVIP (Heavy Vehicle Incentive Program)
- **Funding:** Vouchers ranging from **$60,000 to $330,000** per vehicle.
- **Focus:** Point-of-sale discounts for electric medium and heavy-duty trucks.
- **Deadline:** Ongoing until funds are exhausted for the 2026 cycle.

## Pennsylvania Small Business Advantage Grant (SBAG)
- **Funding:** Up to **$12,000** (reimbursement).
- **Focus:** Energy-efficient upgrades, including **truck retrofits** and aerodynamic improvements for fleets.
- **Deadline:** Applications for the 2025-2026 cycle close **March 13, 2026**.
