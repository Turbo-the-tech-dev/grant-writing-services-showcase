# California SBA STEP Grant

## Overview

The State Trade Expansion Program (STEP) is a U.S. Small Business Administration initiative that funds state-level export assistance programs. In California, STEP support is administered through the Governor's Office of Business and Economic Development and is relevant for small businesses that want to expand sales into international markets.

This opportunity is not a direct SBA award to a small business. Instead, California operates the business-facing program, generally through export vouchers, trade event support, training, and related export-readiness activities.

## Focus

**Primary Goal:** Help California small businesses enter or expand in export markets.

**Common Supported Uses:**

- International market development
- Export training and readiness support
- Trade show or trade mission participation
- Translation and localization
- International digital marketing
- E-commerce globalization and export-oriented website upgrades

## Eligibility

**Typical Business Requirements:**

- For-profit business
- Organized in the United States
- Significant operations in California
- In business for at least one full year
- In good standing with relevant California agencies
- Meets SBA small business size standards for its NAICS code
- Product or service includes at least 51 percent U.S. content
- Able to pay costs upfront before reimbursement

**Operational Fit Notes:**

- Strong fit for productized online businesses with exportable services or goods
- Better fit when the business can document export readiness, target markets, and internal capacity
- Reimbursement structure means cash flow matters

## Funding

**Model:** Reimbursement-based support through California's STEP program.

**Common Support Types:**

- Export vouchers
- STEP events or subsidized trade participation

**Working Amount Reference:** Export voucher support is commonly described as up to `$10,000`, but round-specific terms should be confirmed from the active California program materials before advising a client.

## Deadline

**Status:** Rolling or round-based depending on the California STEP cycle.

**Action Required:** Confirm current deadlines, event windows, and reimbursement rules from the active California STEP portal before logging a live submission target.

## Best-Fit Client Profile

- California small business with at least one year of operations
- Online or e-commerce business with international growth potential
- Company with a clear export-ready product or service
- Business that can show U.S. content, market strategy, and documentation discipline
