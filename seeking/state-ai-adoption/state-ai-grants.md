# State-Level AI Adoption Grants (2026)

Grants for adopting existing AI technologies to improve business operations.

## New Jersey AI for Small Business
- **Funding:** Grants from **$10,000 to $1,000,000**.
- **Focus:** Adopting AI technologies for small business growth.

## Michigan Industry 4.0 Grant
- **Funding:** Up to **$25,000** (50% reimbursement).
- **Focus:** AI hardware/software implementation in manufacturing or logistics.

## Detroit Small Business Technology Fund
- **Funding:** **$1,000 microgrants**.
- **Focus:** Small businesses to adopt AI platforms.
